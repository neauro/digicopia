About

I believe in useful beautiful things. I dream to push the boundaries of technologies and the limitations of usability today, yet build with reality always in consideration. It’s out of the box thinking for real world application.

I live to be creative. My background is in Industrial Design, so when I am not deep in a UX contract I spend much of my free time working in my shop on DIY projects, and small builds. I enjoy exploring various methods of molding, casting and forming for the ever growing list of ID projects. For many of my DIY projects I blog and share process for those with similar interests. For example, check out my Daft Punk Helmet build or my DIY wood pallet shoe rack that was featured on REDDIT.
